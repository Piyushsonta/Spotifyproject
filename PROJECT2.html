Kimetsu no Yaiba is a popular Japanese
manga and anime series created by Koyoharu Gotouge.
The story is set in Taisho-era Japan and follows a
young boy named Tanjiro Kamado, who becomes
demon slayer after his family is slaughtered by demons and
his sister Nezuko is turned into one. The series is
known for its compelling characters, stunning animation,
and emotional depth.
